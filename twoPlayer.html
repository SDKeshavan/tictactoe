How to play?
TicTacToe is a fun game which consists of two playes X and O who compete to capture consequtive three grids Either it can be horizontal, vertical or Diagonal. The corresponding player will be chosen automatically and you need to just pick a position on the 3x3 grid and the first player to get the first three in the same row or column or any diagonal is the winner.
